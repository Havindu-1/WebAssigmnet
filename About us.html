Who we are

Welcome to EpicArcade, your go-to hub for all things gaming! We’re a team of gaming enthusiasts who came together in 2020 with one goal: to create a space where gamers can find everything they need. From honest reviews and expert tips to community discussions, we’re here to celebrate the games we all love. It all started as a group of friends sharing gaming stories and debating which titles deserve a place in the hall of fame. Over time, our passion for gaming grew into something bigger – a website designed to bring players together, whether you’re a casual gamer or a competitive pro. At EpicArcade, we believe gaming is more than just a hobby it’s a lifestyle.

OUR MISSION.

At Epic Arcade, our mission is to unite gamers from all walks of life by providing a one-stop destination for everything gaming.
We aim to empower players with insightful game reviews, expert tips, and a thriving community where everyone feels welcome.
We’re here to celebrate the games that bring us together, inspire creativity, and spark competition. Whether you’re exploring vast open worlds,
crushing opponents in fast-paced battles, or diving into retro classics, we’ve got something for you.



Our VISION.


We envision a future where Epic Arcade is the go-to platform for gamers worldwide—a space where players can connect,
compete, and grow together. We’re dedicated to shaping a gaming culture that’s inclusive, innovative, and endlessly fun.

Our Main Branch

Epic Arcade is the ultimate destination for gamers of all kinds! Located in the heart of the city, our store features an extensive collection of retro arcade machines, the latest gaming consoles, and high-performance gaming PCs.
We also offer a VR zone where you can dive into fully immersive virtual worlds.
Our in-store café, “Pixel Bites,” serves gamer-themed snacks and drinks, like Energy XP Smoothies and Power-Up Burgers.
We host weekly tournaments, live streams, and game release parties. At Epic Arcade, it’s not just about games—it’s about building a community of passionate players!
